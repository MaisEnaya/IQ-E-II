Brush Stroke

Inspired by Ellie & Aster Painting Scene from The Half of It, Brush Stroke is an asynchronous collaborative artwork experience. In here you can see the artwork generated by the simple act of many people creating random brushstrokes, generating abstract art peaces.


    Use the button installation to add a new brush stroke.


    Press "s" to save the current canvas, Escape or "r" to reset canvas.
